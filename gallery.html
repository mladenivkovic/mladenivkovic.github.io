Solution of the Test 4 of Iliev et al 2006 using GEAR-RT, a radiative transfer solver in SWIFT . The test consists of 16 radiating sources in a static cosmological density field heating and ionizing the gas. The movie shows the evolution of the neutral and ionized mass fractions of hydrogen along with the gas temperature projected along the z axis of the entire simulation box.
